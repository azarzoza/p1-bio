Major:

I am a Visual Communication Design major with a minor in Communications. VCD is my major because I want to find a career in the design field and I have really come to enjoy the path I have chosen. I have always wanted to go into book or magazine publication and I think that having a background in design will help me pursue my career goals. I chose to minor in Communications because it is an area that is not my strongest skill, but I feel it will be extremely important for any path I end up going down. I want to focus on magazine and book layout design, printing and website design. I just transferred to Eastern last year, which is when I declared this major. I am still finding my way through all of the Adobe programs and getting a grasp on design.

Background Information:

I graduated High School in 2010 and I had no idea what I wanted to go to school for, I ended up being undeclared for a while at the University of Alaska Anchorage. I thought I wanted to be a Business major for a while, then I took a career exploration course and that really helped me narrow my focus to a degree within Communications.
